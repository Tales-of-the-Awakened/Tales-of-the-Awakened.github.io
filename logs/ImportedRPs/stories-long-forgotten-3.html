Through the masks, the Tenno could smell the air- it was not an unpleasantly stench-filled atmosphere, but rather a stale, mildewy feel that assaulted their nostrils. Each aspect of the ship seemed to be normal besides the swathes of infested growth that coated consoles and machinery alike.

To the left, there was a stripped down modification station which was covered in little clay jars, each of them housing a miniature infested tree. To the right, the foundry, which was currently using some kind of stringy flesh to sew together what appeared to be some kind of Infested weapon. Instead of 3D printing the weapon using a resource synthesizer, three insect-like claws wove the meat-like material together like some kind of unholy tapestry.

Further down, the Companion Modules: the glass done typically used for bathing pets in X-rays to preserve their genetic integrity was down, covered in a spiderweb of cracks. There was nothing inside except what looked like a disembodied heart beating by itself. On the other side, several inactive Djinn sentinels floated lazily around a column of light shining from the ceiling.
