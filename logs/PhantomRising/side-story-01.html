In one practiced movement she vaulted over the guardrail, willing herself to a gradual stop before touching the ground. She took a deep shuddering breath, letting real air fill her rapidly re-materialising lungs as she breached the skein between dimensions. Where there was once no-one stood a tall, willowy young woman, wrapped in dark finery that contrasted sharply with her silver hair. Between her pale blue-tinged pallor, blank ocular implants and golden cybernetic pins, she looked like could have walked straight out of the history books along with the rest of the Vallis' resurrected fauna. Taking a moment to compose herself (and surreptitiously check the load on the ornate revolver at her hip), she strode toward the door, rapping out a short musical sequence with a gloved fist.
