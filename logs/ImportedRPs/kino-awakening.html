~Between broken rock, in the deep stone catacombs of Lua, a room of one of the Veiled rested. This room resembled a prison - crude bars once blocked its entrance on both sides, the Orokin tubular door had been sealed crudely with heavy plates of various metals. All of which, had been torn inward for ominous reasons, leading into a  circular chamber. In the back of the chamber, was a Reservoir Pod.  The room, unreasonably tall, had walls littered with chains, blades, shackles, collars, leg weights and a number of other primitive imprisoning tools dangling absentmindedly. Above was a massive steel sphere, that had protrusion dents all over it, hanging on a thick golden chain. The walls all around the chamber, and even over the many tools, paint laid dry. Orange, black, and various shades of red paint decorated the room's surface and items, smeared everywhere by hands that had taken their time to wipe every single inch. Hand marks littered the floor and ceiling in chaotic patterns, and a single set of footprints led their way to the Reservoir Pod, down the diameter of the room.  Each footstep was outlined with white paint delicately, and in those boundaries a mix of all three colors were slathered by perhaps a brush. The Reservoir Pod beeped quietly; then it beeped, again. The Reservoir Pod beeps once more, and begins to emit a signal. A signal, requesting the pod be opened, echoed through Lua, displaying its location to those who cared to listen, among the broken stone

The tall boy patiently waits, in slumber.~
