Vuko:
   As per usual the Tenno seems to have responded first to Mataeru’s call, almost like he was just waiting for the message. He walks into the conference room with confidence in his step, and a battle-ready look on his face. In addition to his usual Orokin warrior armor apparel there seems to be a beautiful and well taken care of Eisen sword on his right hip. The dark steel blade shimmering in the light as he passes on the different lights, looking like it just has been polished. The hilt gently reflecting the floor and walls in its almost rose gold hue, and the grip neatly covered in dark, navy blue leather.

   After waiting a few seconds, and realizing that no one will come as fast as him, he leans back in his chair and begins tapping the pummel of the sword in anticipation. ‘clang‘ His metal glove hitting the metal ‘cling’ in a rhythmic tone. ‘clunk’(edited)

Isa:
Isa arrived a few minutes after the message had been sent out. She quietly entered the room, remaining near the door for a second, her yellow eyes darting about, taking in the surroundings. She was as expressionless as usual, her face a stone slab that gave no hints as to what was going on within her head.

She walked in further now, stretching her right hand, then forming a fist, then stretching it again, she sat down in the left-most chair of the middle row, rolling her shoulder as she did so. She took a deep breath, then released it. The movements of her left arm stopped as she moved her hands onto her lap and sat looking straight ahead.

Devros von Inie:
A short while after Isa’s entrance, the door to the room hisses once more to reveal another familiar face. There is Devros, the beefy messy haired, glasses wearing boy standing by the doors with the left side of his head wrapped up in bandages – ear and eye alike. Other than that, he appears it spit condition with his Verida suit polished and cleaned and ready for action. Standing next to him is his behemoth of a companion and he seems to be holding something edible in his left hand.

Devros will smile at anyone that looks towards him and walks in, his boots landing with heavy thumps on the floor as if a mountain was on the march. The boy lifts his left hand and takes a juicy bite off what looks like to be a pickled condor chick as if it were a apple, his teeth sinking hard in to the newborns belly with a loud squishy munch, tearing it open like a savage beast.

Mouth full, he plops down to the chair next to Vuko, Garhul occupying the space between the two Tenno. Devros glances towards the Ivara user, with his one currently working eye, and nods at him with a grin, the flesh still swirling inside his maw and slightly poking out from between his teeth.

“Gran’?”

He manages to slur out inbetween chews before gulping down and taking another bite, this time tearing off the chicks left wing and lowering his sullied hand to dangle off the armrest as he munches.

Garhul sits with ease but hoists his head to look towards Vuko, glancing at the boy with a curious look in his dull golden eyes.(edited)

Kapak:

The twisted corpse of a Warframe slithered across the floor like a drunken serpent, almost seeming like he was made of liquid as he appeared to melt over and onto the nearest chair.

Disruptis:

All sorts of lines and small orokin runes rapidly spun in the air along the glow’s surface, accompanied by a quiet, low-pitched humming. Upon noticing the chairs, Disruptis froze mid-step. After a second or two, He quickly scurried up to one and placed a hand on its back. The Tenno pushed down on it, applying gentle pressure, but found it immovable. A minute later, he re-entered the room carrying a light-weight chair. The chair featured a long headrest and tucked-in footrest. Still without a sound, the Limbo set down the chair to the left side of the seating arrangement at the very front. His hands slid to the side of his recliner and pressed a button, causing the foot-rest to swing outwards and allowing the Tenno to lay back in his seat. He promptly did so, picking up an unidentifiable drink which slid out of the arms of the chair.(edited)

~As Mata finished his briefing, the sound of heavy footsteps could be heard moving fast towards the command room.  As the doors slid open, a rather large Hydroid sprinted into the room, not even trying to be subtle about his entrance.~

Muro weighed his options as he charged into the room, swearing at himself with every step. Under ordinary circumstances, he would never have been late for a meeting like this, but what started as a simple errand for his Cephalon turned into a brawl with a patrolling Grineer squad that took far longer than it had any right to. The way he saw it, he had two options now: either he could apologize profusely as he ran, possible disrupting the meeting more than he already has, or just stay silent and just take whatever punishment Mata would have lined up for him later. Deciding on the latter, he practically slid into the leftmost chair in the front row, bringing his Fragor out from its holster at the base of his spine so he could be seated. As he settled into his chair, he brought the hammer down  head first as gently as he could, resting it between his legs.  Muro looked up towards Mata, feeling his leader's icy gaze upon him. he searched for words to explain himself, but nothing satisfactory came up.  All he could do was hang his head after a moment of what would have passed for eye contact between two Warframes. Whatever the mission was, he would have to find out for himself later.

Disruptis:
